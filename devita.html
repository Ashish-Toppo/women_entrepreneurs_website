About Roshni Nadar Malhotra

Devita Saraf is a trailblazing entrepreneur and the driving force behind Vu Televisions, a leading consumer electronics brand in India. With a passion for innovation and a keen eye for design, Devita founded Vu Technologies in 2006 with a vision to disrupt the Indian television market. Armed with a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration from the University of Southern California, she embarked on a journey to redefine the television viewing experience by offering premium-quality products at affordable prices. Devita's hands-on approach and commitment to excellence have propelled Vu Televisions to great heights, earning the brand widespread acclaim and recognition. Her entrepreneurial journey serves as an inspiration to aspiring business leaders, particularly women, as she continues to make her mark in the competitive world of consumer electronics.

Overall, Devita Saraf's visionary leadership, innovative spirit, and unwavering commitment to quality have been instrumental in Vu Televisions' success story. From a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration at the University of Southern California to becoming the Founder, CEO, and Design Head of Vu Televisions, Devita Saraf's journey is a testament to entrepreneurial vision, determination, and innovation. Born into a family with a legacy in the consumer electronics industry, Devita's early exposure to the business world laid the foundation for her entrepreneurial aspirations. Through her visionary leadership and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction, Devita has not only transformed Vu Televisions into a leading brand but has also inspired aspiring entrepreneurs, particularly women, to pursue their dreams and make a mark in the competitive world of business.
