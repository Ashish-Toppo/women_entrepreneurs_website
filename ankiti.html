About Nikiti Bose

Ankiti Bose is a dynamic entrepreneur and the co-founder of Zilingo, a Singapore-based e-commerce platform specializing in fashion and lifestyle products. She co-founded Zilingo in 2015 with Dhruv Kapoor, aiming to provide small merchants in Southeast Asia access to technology and fashion supply chains. Ankiti's background includes work as an investment analyst at Sequoia Capital and as a management consultant at McKinsey & Company. She has garnered recognition as one of the youngest female CEOs leading a startup valued at over a billion dollars. Ankiti is celebrated for her innovative approach to e-commerce and her commitment to empowering small businesses.

Ankiti's contribution to Zilingo

Ankiti Bose's contributions to Zilingo, the Singapore-based e-commerce platform, reflect her multifaceted leadership and unwavering commitment to driving innovation and empowering small businesses. Since co-founding Zilingo in 2015, Ankiti has played a pivotal role in shaping the company's strategic direction and guiding its growth trajectory. Her visionary leadership has been instrumental in propelling Zilingo to become a prominent player in the e-commerce industry, particularly in the fashion and lifestyle sector. Ankiti's strategic acumen and business development expertise have enabled Zilingo to forge strategic partnerships and expand its market reach across Southeast Asia and beyond. Under her stewardship, Zilingo has leveraged cutting-edge technology and digital platforms to enhance the user experience and streamline operations, positioning the company for sustainable growth and success. Ankiti's deep commitment to empowering small merchants and entrepreneurs has been at the heart of Zilingo's mission, as the platform continues to provide opportunities for businesses to thrive in the ever-evolving digital landscape. Her passion for driving positive change and making a meaningful impact has earned her recognition as one of the most influential leaders in the e-commerce ecosystem.

Ankiti Bose: Leading Zilingo

Ankiti Bose's journey to success is marked by resilience, determination, and a relentless pursuit of her entrepreneurial vision. Growing up in India, Ankiti faced numerous challenges and societal expectations, but she remained undeterred in her pursuit of her dreams. After graduating from the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IIT) with a degree in Mathematics and Computer Science, Ankiti embarked on a career path that would ultimately lead her to co-found Zilingo. However, her journey was far from easy. As a young woman navigating the male-dominated tech and business world, Ankiti encountered skepticism and obstacles at every turn. Despite facing rejections and setbacks, she remained steadfast in her belief in the transformative power of technology and entrepreneurship to drive positive change.

Undeterred by the challenges, Ankiti, along with her co-founder Dhruv Kapoor, took a leap of faith and founded Zilingo in 2015. The early days of Zilingo were fraught with uncertainties and challenges, from securing funding to building a team and gaining traction in a competitive market. Ankiti and Dhruv worked tirelessly, often sacrificing personal comforts and financial security, to turn their vision into reality. They hustled to bootstrap the company, knocking on doors and pitching their idea to potential investors and partners. Despite facing numerous rejections, Ankiti and Dhruv remained resilient, driven by their unwavering belief in the potential of Zilingo to revolutionize the e-commerce landscape.

Through sheer determination and perseverance, Ankiti overcame the odds and steered Zilingo towards success. Today, Zilingo stands as a testament to Ankiti's indomitable spirit and entrepreneurial vision, empowering small merchants and entrepreneurs across Southeast Asia and beyond. Ankiti's journey from facing skepticism and challenges to founding a billion-dollar company serves as an inspiration to aspiring entrepreneurs around the world, demonstrating the power of resilience, determination, and unwavering belief in one's dreams.
