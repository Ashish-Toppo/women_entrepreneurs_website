Roshni Nadar Malhotra: Leading HCL Technologies and Transforming Education

Roshni Nadar Malhotra's journey to success is a testament to her exceptional leadership, vision, and dedication to driving positive change. Born into a family renowned for its entrepreneurial spirit, Roshni was exposed to the world of business and innovation from a young age. Roshni's early years at HCL were marked by her commitment to learning and innovation. She immersed herself in various roles within the company, gaining valuable insights into the intricacies of the tech industry. Her passion for technology and her strategic mindset quickly caught the attention of HCL's leadership, leading to her rapid ascent through the ranks.

In 2009, Roshni was appointed to the Board of Directors of HCL Technologies, marking a significant milestone in her career. Over the years, she played a pivotal role in shaping the company's growth strategy, driving innovation, and expanding its global footprint. Under her visionary leadership, the company has embraced digital disruption, expanded its portfolio of services, and forged strategic partnerships with leading global organizations.
