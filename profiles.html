Women Empowering Women

Some of the most popular and influential Women Intrepreneurs of India are listed below.

Roshni Nadar Malhotra

Roshni Nadar Malhotra is the Chairperson of HCL Technologies, making her the first woman to lead a listed Indian IT company. She is also the founder and trustee of The Habitats Trust, an organization dedicated to protecting India's natural habitats and species.

Ankita Bose

Ankiti Bose is the co-founder and former CEO of Zilingo, a fashion and lifestyle e-commerce platform that gained significant traction in Southeast Asia. She is recognized for being one of the youngest female entrepreneurs to lead a startup valued at nearly $1 billion.

Read More!
Devita Saraf

Devita Saraf is the founder and CEO of Vu Televisions, one of India's leading premium television brands, known for her innovative approach and leadership in the technology industry. She has been recognized as one of the most influential women in business in India.
