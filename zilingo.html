Zilingo

Zilingo, founded in 2015 by Ankiti Bose and Dhruv Kapoor, has quickly emerged as a disruptive force in the fashion and lifestyle e-commerce industry. Based in Singapore, Zilingo operates as a technology platform that connects fashion merchants and suppliers with consumers, providing a seamless and engaging shopping experience. The company's innovative approach to e-commerce has democratized access to fashion, enabling small merchants and independent designers to reach a global audience.

At the heart of Zilingo's success is its commitment to leveraging technology to address inefficiencies in the traditional fashion supply chain. Through its platform, Zilingo offers a wide range of fashion and lifestyle products, from clothing and accessories to beauty and home decor, sourced directly from manufacturers and suppliers. This direct-to-consumer model not only ensures competitive pricing but also promotes transparency and sustainability in the fashion industry.

Furthermore, Zilingo's focus on data-driven insights and personalized recommendations enhances the shopping experience for consumers, driving engagement and loyalty. With a strong emphasis on customer satisfaction and seller empowerment, Zilingo continues to innovate and expand its presence in key markets, cementing its position as a leading player in the global e-commerce landscape.
